App Features

Real-Time Flight Data

Track your flight's ground speed, altitude, and location in real-time, even in Airplane Mode.

Offline Map Caching

Pre-load maps for your route before the flight and access them offline, giving you detailed navigation throughout your journey.

Auto-Update Position

Flight Mode Map automatically updates your location during the flight, allowing you to see your progress in real time.

Easy to Use

The app is designed for simplicity, so even when you're offline, you can rely on it without any hassle.
