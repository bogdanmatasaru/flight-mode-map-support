Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cache the map for offline use?

Before your flight, open the map and zoom in and out to cache it. You’ll be able to use the map offline during your flight.

Why isn’t my GPS working?

GPS signals can be weak inside the aircraft. Try sitting near a window for better results.
